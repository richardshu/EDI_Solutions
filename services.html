Full Service EDI

Rapidly implement your EDI at a guaranteed low cost. Fully managed, completely hands off, and full service EDI solutions and services. No EDI software required. No EDI training required. No extra computer hardware required. All you need is an email address.

EDI Solutions is an approved EDI solutions provider for major retailers. We provide express EDI implementations with all major retailers. In most cases, your EDI will be up and running in 1-3 days. EDI Solutions service is NOT a bothersome web-based EDI solution which requires you to login and do the work yourself.

Here's a breakdown of our full service pipeline:

  • EDI Solutions establishes an EDI connection with your trading partner and receives your documents automatically.
  • We translate EDI raw data into a human readable report and email the report to you.
  • We send a return receipt to your trading partner to acknowledge the receipt of their documents (e.g. purchase orders).
  • Documents you wish to transmit to your EDI trading partners are emailed to us in an Excel file.
  • Your invoice document is processed and sent to your trading partner in EDI format.
  • We receive return confirmation from your trading partner as evidence of their receipt of your documents.
  • We print and ship UCC-128 barcode carton shipping labels or UPC tickets to you if needed.

AS2 EDI-INT Setup

EDI Solutions will configure and set up EDI-INT AS2 software, conduct testing AS2 with your trading partner, and train your EDI personnel to use this state-of-the-art EDI connection standard. This new communication protocol uses the Internet to transmit EDI data rather than using traditional value-added networks. This allwos network transmission charges to be eliminated. Walmart, for instance, requires their EDI trading partners to use this new technology.
