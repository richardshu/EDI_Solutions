EDI Solutions establishes an EDI connection with your trading partner and receive your documents for you automatically. AS2 EDI-INT set up

EDI Solutions will configure and set up EDI-INT AS2 software, conduct testing AS2 with your trading partner and train your EDI personnel to use this state-of-the-art EDI connection standard. This new communication protocol uses internet as a vehicle to transmit EDI data instead of using traditional value added networks. Network transmission charges can be eliminated. The largest retailer of the world, Wal-Mart is requiring their EDI trading partner to use this new technology.
